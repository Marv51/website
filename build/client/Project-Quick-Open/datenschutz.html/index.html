Privacy Policy for Project-Quick-Open

Project-Quick-Open is an application developed by me, Marvin Rühe. It is distributed exclusively through the Microsoft Store.

The Project-Quick-Open app does not send us any of your personal data. In fact it does not connect to the internet at all.

Additionally, please notice that the download and purchase of this app is handled by the Microsoft Store. If you have any questions about your privacy there, please check their policy.
